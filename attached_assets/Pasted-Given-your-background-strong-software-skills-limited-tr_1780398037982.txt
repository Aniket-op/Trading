### What I would actually build

If your objective is a BTC perpetual futures bot with a realistic chance of surviving live trading, I would prioritize:

1. **Freqtrade** as the first execution layer (faster than building LEAN integration).
2. HMM regime detection.
3. Kalman trend filter.
4. Entropy filter.
5. Bayesian confidence score.
6. Volatility-targeted sizing.
7. Half-Kelly cap.
8. Walk-forward validation.
9. Monte Carlo stress testing.
10. Only then move to live capital.

The biggest mistake most developers make is spending 80% of effort on signals and 20% on risk. Profitable systematic trading is usually closer to the opposite: data quality, risk management, position sizing, and execution determine whether a statistically decent signal actually survives in production.
